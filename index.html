Welcome!

What is Meribah Books?

Meribah Book is the publishing site for the fiction of Linda Watson Smith. It is the culmination of a dream that began in childhood. In 1994 that dream was made real with completion of her first novel, which found its home here in 2020. All of the stories found on this site are offered free of charge with the hope that they will at least entertain and at most bless any and all readers.

Crash Course: A Parker and Johnson Story

Parker is a private detective living on the road with her partner and canine companion, Johnson. Parker accepts a job of finding a runaway, never expecting that the events to follow would blow open her own hidden and dangerous past. Maybe the time has come to stop running.

Siblings: A Flash Fiction Duo

Two flash fiction stories, each about siblings and each told entirely in dialogue. In “Sibling Teleportation” a brother and sister come up with a plan to be together while apart in order to help each other through family crisis. “Jump” tells the humorous story of a brother trying to resist his sister’s latest birthday challenge.
